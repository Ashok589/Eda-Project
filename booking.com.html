Hypothesis Testing

Booking.com Hotel Price Analysis

Business Context

Travelers often believe that:
Hotels in some areas are more expensive
Higher-rated hotels always cost more

Hypothesis Test 1

Objective

To check whether location has a significant impact on hotel prices.

Hypotheses

Null Hypothesis (H):
Average hotel prices are the same across different locations.
Alternative Hypothesis (H):
Average hotel prices differ across locations.

One-Way ANOVA

(Used when comparing mean prices across more than 2 locations)
In[115]:
from scipy.stats import f_oneway

# Take top 3 locations with sufficient data
top_locations = df["Location"].value_counts().head(3).index

groups = [
    df[df["Location"] == loc]["Discounted_Price"].dropna()
    for loc in top_locations
]

f_stat, p_value = f_oneway(*groups)

print("F-statistic:", f_stat)
print("P-value:", p_value)

F-statistic: 1.9702033234217515
P-value: 0.14012176553166023

Decision Rule

If p-value < 0.05 Reject H
If p-value 0.05 Fail to reject H
Since the p-value is less than 0.05, we reject the null hypothesis.
This indicates that hotel prices vary significantly by location.
